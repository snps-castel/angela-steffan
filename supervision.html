Systemic Supervision

Professional development through systemic supervision. Supporting therapists, counselors, and helping professionals in their growth and reflective practice.

Systemic supervision provides a structured space for reflection, learning, and professional growth. Through a collaborative approach, we explore your practice, challenges, and development as a helping professional.

Focus Areas

  • Case conceptualization and therapeutic relationships
  • Systemic thinking and circular questioning techniques
  • Ethical dilemmas and professional boundaries
  • Self-care and preventing burnout
  • For team and group supervisions, I also come to your teams within a 30km radius.
